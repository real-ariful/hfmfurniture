Integration of SSLCommerz Payment Acquirer with Odoo eCommerce

SSLCOMMERZ is the first payment gateway in Bangladesh opening doors for merchants to receive payments on the internet via their online stores. Customers are able to buy products online using their credit cards as well as bank accounts. If you are a merchant, you have come to the right place! Enhance your business by integrating SSLCOMMERZ to your online store and facilitating online payment in Bangladeshi Taka. Your customers will be able to pay for your products using local credit/debit cards like VISA, MasterCard, DBBL Nexus Card and any kind of credit card or bank accounts right from your online store. SSLCOMMERZ uses industry standard Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology which is used worldwide for securing data encryption.

How to install the SSLCommerz Payment Acquirer

Install on your server:

  1. Download the code on your server.
  2. Also add the module folder path to your Odoo Conf file.
  3. Restart your server.
  4. Go to Settings. Click on "Activate the developer mode".
  5. Go to Apps. Click on "Update Apps List" and then click "Update".
  6. Remove all the filters and search by "bose_ssl_commerz".
  7. Click on Install.

This Odoo Module supports:

SSL Commerz Hosted Payment Integration

Here, you will redirect the customer to SSLCOMMERZ Hosted page to display the payment channels

Card Payment using SSL Commerz Redirect:

Select SSL Commerz in Website and click on "Pay Now"

Add card details and click on "PAY" button at the bottom.

A OTP will be sent to your mobile number. Insert the OTP and then click "Confirm"

Payment successful message along with Transaction details will be shown.

Configuration for App:

There are two configurations:

  1. Configuration for SSLCOMMERZ Panel
  2. Configuration for Odoo

1. Configuration for SSLCOMMERZ Panel

After creation of Store, you will get a mail. Follow the steps below:

  1. Go to mail .Note down your store id and store password. These will required later in Odoo Configuration.
  2. Click on "Merchant Panel URL".
  3. Give Login ID, Password and Capture. Login ID and Password are given in the mail forwarded to you by SSL Commerz.
  4. Your profile will be created.
  5. Click on "My Stores". Your new created store will be shown.
  6. Click on "IPN Settings" in Store Table under Action Tab.

  7. Click on "IPN Settings" in Store Table under Action Tab.

  8. Activate "Enable HTTP Listener". Add Your URL: "https://YOUR_SERVER_URL/payment/sslcommerz/ipn/" Click Save.

2. Configuration for Odoo

  1. Go to Invoicing --> Configuration --> Payment Acuirers
  2. Click on "SSL Commerz Redirect"

    • Change State "Enabled" for use in Production or "Test Mode" for use in Sandbox
    • Select your Website
    • Add your Store ID
    • Add your Store Password

  4. Go to "Configuration" Tab
    • Select Payment Method for example "Bank"
    • Add allowed countries if necessary for you
  5. Save the Record.
